Privacy Policy

How the Dendro mobile app handles your data.

This policy explains how the Dendro mobile application ("the app") handles your data. Dendro is a companion tool for poison dart frog hobbyists.

By installing or using the app you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall.

1.Quick summary

  • The app works 100% offline for your hobby data (frogs, cultures, clutches, photos).
  • There is no user account, no login, no registration.
  • We only share data with third parties for four specific purposes: weather, purchase validation, promotional codes, and technical crash reports.

2.Data stored locally on your device

The following is stored only on your phone (local memory and filesystem). It never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app:

  • Information about your frogs, cultures, clutches and sightings.
  • Photos you take with the in-app camera.
  • App settings (language, theme, notifications, alerts).
  • PRO mode state (purchases, trial, redeemed code).
  • A local installation ID (not linked to your identity).

3.Data sent to third parties

We keep this to a minimum. These are the only cases:

3.1 WeatherAPI.com (weather data)

  • What is sent: the geographic coordinates of the natural habitat of the species you are viewing. NOT your real location. Example: if you view a Dendrobates auratus, we send Panama coordinates.
  • When: only when you open the "Now in your jungle" screen.
  • Policy: weatherapi.com/privacy.aspx

3.2 Our server (purchase validation and promotional codes)

  • What is sent:
    • To validate a purchase: your Google Play purchase token and the product ID.
    • To redeem a promotional code: the code and a random installation ID (not linked to you).
  • When: only when you buy Dendro PRO, restore a purchase, or enter a promotional code.

3.3 Google Play Billing (payments)

  • What is sent: the data required to process your purchase (handled by Google, not by us).
  • When: when you buy Dendro PRO or restore a previous purchase.
  • Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3.4 Firebase Crashlytics (crash reports)

  • What is sent: if the app crashes, an automatic report is sent containing the error stack trace, device model, Android version, and an anonymous Firebase installation ID. Your hobby data and photos are never sent.
  • When: only when a crash occurs.
  • Operator: Google LLC (firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

4.What we do not collect

  • Your real location (GPS / WiFi).
  • Personal identity: name, email, phone, age.
  • Contacts, messages, microphone.
  • User account (there is no registration).
  • Analytics data (Firebase Analytics is disabled).
  • Advertising identifiers.
  • Browsing history or activity outside the app.

5.System permissions requested

  • Camera: optional, only if you want to take photos of your frogs.
  • Storage (gallery): optional, to pick existing photos.
  • Internet: for weather, purchase validation and crash reports.
  • Notifications: optional, for culture and sighting alerts.

All optional permissions are requested when needed, not at install time.

6.Your rights (GDPR / similar)

Although most data stays on your device:

  • Access / portability: export a copy from Settings > Backup > Export.
  • Full deletion: uninstall the app — all local data is removed.
  • Selective deletion: delete individual frogs, cultures or clutches from inside the app.
  • Data held by third parties (our server, Firebase, Google): contact us at thejollysky@gmail.com and we will make reasonable efforts to remove your installation ID from our records. Firebase data is automatically deleted after 90 days by default.
  • California residents (CCPA): you have the right to know what we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

7.Minimum age

Dendro is designed for adults and users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8.Retention

  • Local data: until you uninstall the app.
  • Crash reports in Firebase: 90 days.
  • Purchase data in Google Play: per Google policy.
  • Promotional codes on our server: kept indefinitely (no personal data).

9.Changes to this policy

Any material change will be published on this page and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. We will notify you inside the app for important changes.

10.Contact

For privacy questions:

Jolly Sky — thejollysky@gmail.com

We respond to privacy requests within 30 days.
